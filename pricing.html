Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for larger transactions. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! Both seller and buyer paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What happens if my system doesn't sell?

Your subscription gives you ongoing listing access whether or not your system sells. You only pay the success fee when a sale is completed.
