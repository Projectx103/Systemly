What is Systemly and how does it work?
Systemly is a secure marketplace for buying and selling complete digital systems, automation workflows, and online businesses. We connect verified sellers with buyers, provide secure PayMongo transactions, and facilitate smooth transfers of digital assets. Think of us as the trusted middleman that ensures both parties get what they expect.
How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner, enter your email and create a password, or sign in with Google. After verification, you can immediately browse listings. To buy or sell, complete your profile with additional verification details.
Is Systemly free to use?
Browsing and creating an account is completely free. Sellers pay a small commission (5-10% depending on plan) only when their system sells. Buyers pay no fees. We also offer premium seller plans with enhanced visibility and features.
What types of systems can I find on Systemly?
You'll find e-commerce stores, SaaS businesses, content websites, mobile apps, social media accounts, automation workflows, plugins, digital agencies, AI tools, and more. Every listing includes detailed metrics, revenue data, and asset inventories.
How do I purchase a system?
Browse listings, click on one you're interested in, review all details, and click "Acquire Now." You'll go through our secure checkout process with PayMongo. Funds are held by the system for 24 hours until the seller transfers all assets and you confirm receipt.
How do I know if a listing is legitimate?
All listings are manually reviewed by our team. We verify revenue claims, check asset ownership, and validate seller identity. Look for the "Verified" badge on listings. You can also request additional documentation during due diligence.
Can I negotiate the price?
Yes! Use the "Message Seller" button to start a conversation and make an offer. Many sellers are open to negotiation, especially for quick sales. Our team can also mediate discussions if needed.
What happens after I purchase?
After payment, the seller has 7 days to transfer all listed assets (domains, code, accounts, etc.). You'll receive a transfer checklist to verify. Once you confirm receipt, funds are released to the seller. Our support team guides you through every step.
What if the system doesn't match the description?
If you discover discrepancies during the verification period, contact our support team immediately. We'll investigate and mediate. If the seller misrepresented the system, you're eligible for a full refund through our buyer protection program.
How do I list my system for sale?
Click "Sell Now," complete the listing form with detailed information about your system, upload screenshots and documentation, set your asking price, and submit for review. Our team typically approves listings within 24-48 hours.
How should I price my system?
Use our free AI valuation tool for an estimate based on revenue, profit, age, and market comparables. Typical multiples are 2-4x annual profit for established systems. Price competitively to attract serious buyers while maximizing your return.
What fees do sellers pay?
Free sellers pay 10% commission on successful sales. Basic plan (‚Ç±399/month) reduces this to 7%, and Premium plan (‚Ç±799/month) charges only 5%. Commission is only charged when your system sells, never upfront.
How long does it take to sell?
Average time to sale is 30-60 days for well-priced, documented systems. Premium listings with comprehensive data and active seller engagement sell faster. We'll provide visibility metrics and optimization tips in your seller dashboard.
What should I include in my listing?
Include accurate revenue/profit data, traffic stats, customer information, operational details, included assets (domains, code, accounts), tech stack, and growth opportunities. More transparency = faster sales at better prices. Upload screenshots, analytics, and documentation.
What payment methods do you accept?
We use PayMongo for secure transactions, supporting credit cards, debit cards, GCash, GrabPay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely and held by the system until transfer completion.
How does escrow protection work?
When you purchase, your payment is held securely in escrow. The seller transfers assets, you verify everything matches the listing, and then you approve release. If issues arise, our team investigates before releasing funds. This protects both parties.
When do sellers receive payment?
Funds are released to sellers after the buyer confirms receipt of all assets and the verification period (7 days) completes. Payments are typically processed within 3-5 business days to your chosen payout method.
Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees! Buyers pay zero fees. Sellers pay only the stated commission percentage when their system sells. PayMongo processing fees are included in our commission, so the price you see is what you pay/receive.
Can I get a refund?
Yes, if the system doesn't match the listing description or the seller fails to transfer assets as promised. During the 7-day verification period, you can dispute and request a full refund. Our team reviews all disputes fairly and quickly.
How do I change my password?
Go to your Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Security. Click "Change Password," enter your current password and new password, then save. You'll receive a confirmation email. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
How do I verify my account?
Email verification happens automatically when you sign up. For additional verification (required for selling and large purchases), go to Dashboard ‚Üí Verification and upload a government ID. Verification typically completes within 24 hours.
Can I have multiple accounts?
No, each user is allowed one account. Multiple accounts may be flagged and suspended. If you need separate buyer and seller profiles, both functions are available in a single account. Contact support if you have special business needs.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. You'll need to confirm via email. Note: You cannot delete your account if you have active listings or pending transactions. All data will be permanently deleted after 30 days.
What are the different subscription plans?
Free: Browse and buy unlimited, sell with 10% commission. Basic (‚Ç±999/month): 7% commission, priority support, 5 active listings. Premium (‚Ç±2,999/month): 5% commission, featured listings, unlimited active listings, dedicated account manager, advanced analytics.
Why can't I upload images?
Images must be under 5MB each and in JPG, PNG, or WebP format. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If problems persist, try a different browser, clear your cache, or compress your images before uploading.
What browsers are supported?
Systemly works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience. Mobile browsers are fully supported on both iOS and Android.
Why am I getting logged out frequently?
This usually happens if cookies are disabled or you're in incognito mode. Enable cookies in your browser settings and check "Remember Me" when logging in. Sessions last 30 days by default. Clear your browser cache if issues continue.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Click the "Report Issue" button in your dashboard footer, or email support@systemly.com with details about the problem, what you were doing, and your browser/device. Screenshots are very helpful. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
Is my data secure on Systemly?
Yes! We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Payment data is handled by PayMongo (PCI-DSS compliant). We never share your data with third parties without consent.
