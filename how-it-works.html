How Systemly Works

A simple, secure, and straightforward process for buying and selling digital systems. From listing to transfer, we've got you covered every step of the way.

1

Browse & Discover

Explore our curated marketplace of verified digital systems. Use advanced filters to find systems that match your niche, budget, and goals. Each listing includes detailed analytics, revenue reports, and performance metrics.

Verified Listings Full Analytics Smart Filters
2

Connect & Negotiate

Reach out to sellers through our secure messaging system. Ask questions, request additional information, and negotiate terms. Our platform facilitates transparent communication while protecting both parties' privacy.

Secure Chat Safe Negotiation Clear Terms
3

Secure Payment via PayMongo

Once you agree on terms, funds are placed in our PayMongo system. Your payment is held securely and only released to the seller after successful system transfer and your approval. Complete buyer protection guaranteed.

Funds Protected PayMongo Secure 24-48hr Hold
4

System Transfer & Verification

The seller transfers all system assets, including domains, accounts, content, and documentation. Our team guides both parties through the process, ensuring nothing is missed. You'll have time to verify everything works correctly.

Complete Checklist Expert Support Full Verification
5

Approve & Complete

After verifying the system is fully operational and all assets are transferred, you approve the transaction. Funds are released to the seller, and you receive all documentation. Enjoy ongoing support for 30 days post-purchase.

Your Approval Required 30-Day Support Transaction Complete
1

Create Your Listing

Sign up and submit your digital system for review. Provide detailed information about your system, including revenue data, traffic stats, tech stack, and operations. Our AI-powered valuation tool helps you price it right.

AI Valuation Easy Submission Pricing Guidance
2

Verification & Approval

Our team reviews your system to ensure it meets our quality standards. We verify revenue claims, check system functionality, and ensure all information is accurate. This typically takes 24-48 hours.

Expert Review 24-48 Hours Quality Assured
3

Go Live & Get Offers

Your listing goes live on our marketplace, visible to thousands of qualified buyers. Respond to inquiries, share additional details, and negotiate with interested parties. You're in control of accepting offers.

Maximum Exposure Direct Messaging You Control Offers
4

Accept Offer & PayMongo

When you accept an offer, the buyer's funds are secured in PayMongo. This protects you both. You'll receive a detailed transfer checklist and timeline. Our support team guides you through every step of the transfer process.

Secure PayMongo Transfer Guide Dedicated Support
5

Transfer & Get Paid

Complete the system transfer following our comprehensive checklist. Once the buyer verifies and approves, funds are released to your account within 24 hours. Celebrate your successful sale!

Fast Payment Buyer Approval Deal Complete
